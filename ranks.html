My 5 Favorite Games

Below are my personal favorite games/game series that I have played. There's also a form after where you can let me know what your favorite games are. I consider myself an
avid gamer so I'm always open to being introduced to new games!

  1. Persona 5 Royal
  2. The Legend of Heroes: Trails Series
  3. The Mass Effect Trilogy
  4. Final Fantasy XIV
  5. Heroes of Might and Magic V

Explaining the Ranks

This game was really fun to play. Its artstyle is very unique and I enjoyed the game mechanics. I've played through the almost 100 hour story twice and have lost many afternoons
and nights playing this game.

In terms of worldbuilding, this JRPG series takes the cake in my book. Honestly, I consider this series and the persona series equal. The only reason this is 2nd on the
list is because Persona 5 was the first JRPG I fully devoted myself to, and the rest is history.

Mass Effect is a really good Sci-Fi trilogy (we don't talk about Andromeda.). I was almost immediately hooked and absorbed into the world and became invested in the
story and characters. There is so much replayability as well, as the choices you make shape the story.

This MMO is really fun, to say the least. You can play through so much content through the free trial alone, and it's definitely even more fun to play with friends. The
game mechanics aren't to hard to grasp initially, though I think the skill ceiling can seem pretty high to someone who's never played an MMO before.

This game will always hold a special place. This game, that probably not a lot of people my age have heard of, was the first game I ever remember playing. Growing up I
would watch my dad play games and I vividly remember him playing this when I was still in preschool. I revist this game from time to time whenever I need some nostalgia.
